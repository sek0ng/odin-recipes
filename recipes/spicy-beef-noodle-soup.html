Spicy Beef Noodle Soup

Description

It features a robust clear broth infused with rich spices and a spicy kick. Soft noodles soak in the flavorful broth, accompanied by vegetables and protein. What sets this dish apart is its delightful texture and diverse flavors.

Ingredients

    Broth
    Rice noodles
    Sliced beef
    Tofu
    Spices

Steps

    Prepare the Broth
    Cook the beef
    Cook the rice noodles
    Add Vegetables and Seasonings
    Pour the Broth
